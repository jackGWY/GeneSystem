Colorectal cancer (CRC), also known as bowel cancer and colon cancer, is the development of cancer from the colon or rectum (parts of the large intestine).A cancer is the abnormal growth of cells that have the ability to invade or spread to other parts of the body. Signs and symptoms may include blood in the stool, a change in bowel movements, weight loss, and feeling tired all the time.

Human Colon Cancer Database extracts gene, pathway, network and drug information (including its chemical structure and principle of action) from the existing literature and data of various platforms, and systematically and structurically integrate the main mechanism and therapeutic drugs for the occurrence of colon cancer. The online human colon cancer database system can effectively compensate for the deficiency of the existing database, and provide convenience for researchers in the field of colon cancer to search for relevant information.
